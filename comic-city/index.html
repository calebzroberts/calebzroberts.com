Lancaster, PA's favorite family comic book store!

Comic City is Lancaster’s favorite family Comic Book Store! Our staff have a passion for the entire comic book industry that few can match, which is why we’ve dedicated ourselves and our store to bringing you the finest comic book experience in all of Central PA!
