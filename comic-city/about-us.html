Our Story

A little About Us

Comic City is Lancaster’s favorite family comic book store! Our staff has a passion for the entire comic book industry that few can match, which is why we’ve dedicated ourselves and our store to bringing you the finest comic book experience in all of Central PA!

Our grand opening fills a much-needed role as a hub for all the “nerdy types” that call Lancaster home. As our wonderfully weird and unique customer base grows, we moved into our first location—a spacious building, tripling our space and our ability to deliver geeky goodness!

One of the great pleasures of working at Comic City is getting to know our customers, both the occasional and the faithful weekly shoppers. Lancaster is a wonderfully diverse, unique town—you never know who will walk through the door next!

Our Mission

To provide our customers with a unique and remarkable shopping experience that confounds the expectations of what is typically associated with a comic shop;

To act as ambassadors for all things geeky and to present our wares as the awesome, fun, and universally accessible pieces of entertainment that they are.

We have all the newest comic releases but we offer so much more than just comics. Stop by and see all the graphic novels, collectibles, miniatures, role playing games, collectible card games, t-shirts, board games and more.
