Meet the Teacher

Mr. Hess
With 30 years in the design industry and 13 years spent at Brownstown CTC, Mr. Hess has helped shape the future of countless aspiring designers. A few notable projects in the Intro to Visual Communications program include the Cereal Box Project and Season’s Greeting Cards. These projects enable students to develop skills and find success in design. Outside of CTC, Mr. Hess enjoys being outdoors by flying drones, motorcycling and kayaking. Above all, Mr. Hess is a proud grandfather to two grandsons, whom he loves and takes on exciting adventures.
