Meet the Teacher

Mr. Baird
Mr. Baird has been at the Brownstown CTC for 22 years and had experience in advertising for 10 years prior. In the commercial art class, you can expect to work on media like brochures, posters, advertisements, 3d packaging and more. One of the major projects to look forward to is the Maquette, which is a small 3d model prototype of a character. Mr. Baird has been married for 27 years and has 2 children. Outside of his work at CTC, he enjoys reading, podcasts, outdoor activities like camping and canoeing, and visiting art museums.
